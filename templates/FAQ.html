Students Help

I don’t know my login credentials ?
You can login to your account using your school Learner Reference Number (LRN) and for the password Lastname and birthdate.

San Juan Elementary School Login credentials format:
ID [2000 . last five digits of your ID Number]
Password: Last name + birthdate (YYYYMMDD)

Sample:
ID:20010121
Password:Sanjuan20010121

*Passwords are case sensitive, so make sure you enter it with the first letter of your last name in UPPERCASE.

I forgot my password ?
You may contact our mail through our feedback page. For the meantime contact your adviser for assistance.
I have missing subjects ?
Please inform your school to update your class assignment. Further, you may send an email at feedback page
